How Packages Work

It's pretty simple. You order a Package, and we bring it to you. When we receive your order, we will text you to figure out delivery details. While we will always try to get Packages to you as fast as we can, Thursdays are the only night we promise on-demand delivery. You can order any day of the week, and we'll figure out a delivery time that works for both of us.
