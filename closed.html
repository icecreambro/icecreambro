how it works.

you're tired. you're stressed. it is thursday night and you're trying to finish your homework, but your motivation is dwindling. sometimes all you need is a cold, creamy treat to get you through the night. that's where ice cream bro comes in. within minutes of placing an order on our site, we will bring you a 1 Pint container of blue bunny ice cream. your thursday nights will never be the same.

hours of operation: thurs 8:30p-2:30a

about us.

ice cream bro is made up of two lincoln freshman, jacob shiohira (@jshiohaha) and reid gahan (@reidgahan). we both love ice cream, but hate having to drive to the store to buy some. once our friends echoed our complaints, we decided to do something about it. if you feel the same way we do, you have come to the right place! classes are hard. weeks are long. homework is boring. but life at unl just got a little bit better with ice cream bro - a business founded on a love for ice cream and a desire to put a smile on your face.
